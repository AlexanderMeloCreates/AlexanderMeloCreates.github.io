How do I see myself?

What have I done?

What do I want to accomplish?

I am unreasonably passionate about video games and have dreamed since childhood of making my own.

To get there, I have learned how to program, create digital art, animate, produce music, and tell stories. I endeavour to be an interdisciplinary artist, and I grow these skills at every opportunity.

My fervency for independent developing doesn't mean I believe I have gotten this far on my own. Lucky circumstances and gracious people have paved my path. In time, I hope that I can return the favour back onto the world.

In 2023, I will graduate with a Bachelor's Degree in the Creative Industries and a minor in Computer Science from Toronto Metropolitan University.

I completed an Internship at Autonomicity Games, where the bulk of my work went into building and launching their new website.

I am many years into developing Sunkshore City, my first indie game.

I want to release Sunkshore City, the game I am currently developing, on Steam.

All art asks something of the audience. In games, most ask for money, some ask for time, dedication, and mastery, while others ask for emotional investment.

My favourite games ask the audience to think critically of the game and its characters, before reflecting back onto the self. This is the kind of game I strive to make.

If that interests you, you can (soon) wishlist the game here, on Steam.
